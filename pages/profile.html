About Andrew Kendall

I grew up in Braunston, a small village in Northamptonshire. At the age of 18 I took a year out from university to work with the internet, when I applied again a year later instead of being offered a place on the course I was offered a job as a 'Digital Media' lecturer at the London Institute. I began lecturing at 19 and have been working there since.

I've also been documenting my life via photography since 1997, in 2003 after taking photographs and putting them online I was contacted by the NME and am now working with them and various other publications as music photographer.

If you would like to give me feedback on my site, hire me for a job or ask me any other questions please feel free to contact me.
